Here is some practical information
so that your Team Building Experience is as nice as possible

  • The morning will be spent indoors so it will be like a regular office day, nothing special to prepare
  • Afterwards we will go out for lunch so perhaps wear something with a loose waist band so you can eat a lot (just kidding... not really ;)
  • Finally we will do some bouldering (indoor rock climbing) so bring some comfy loose clothes you can change into (tshirt and leggings/shorts/comfy pants)
  • bouldering is a messy activity with powder everywhere so don't bring a very nice tshirt, the ratty one with holes from the back of your closet will be the most ideal!
  • Footwear: We will rent climbing shoes on the spot so you don't need to bring anything in particular
